I started my career as a Software Engineering Teacher at SMK Bintang Harapan, where I discovered my passion for technology and teaching. This foundation led me to become a Backend Developer at Padepokan Tujuh Sembilan, where I worked on payroll systems and HRIS applications.

For the past 7+ years, I've been with Telkom Indonesia, where I've grown from a Backend Developer to a Technical Lead, managing complex projects like Indibiz Pay, Q Ren, and Umeetme.id. I've led teams, architected microservices, and delivered solutions that handle millions of transactions.
